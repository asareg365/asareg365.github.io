George Oppong Asare

Administrative Assistant

George Oppong Asare was born on 2nd May 1987 at Berekum in the Bono Region of Ghana, 1.78m tall and dark in complexion. He completed his basic school at Voice of Deliverance Preparatory School in Berekum in 2003, he then proceeded to Sunyani Senior High School for his High School education and completed in the year 2006. He entered into family business with his mother and he was selling sanitary products in their family shop. In the year 2009 he gained admission into the University for Development studies- Wa to study Bachelor of Arts in Integrated Community Development. He graduated with a Second Class Lower in 2013. George started his National Service at the Department of Social Welfare and Community Development Goaso Municipal Assembly, he did immensely well leading to him been adjourned the Best Service Personnel in Volunteerism in the whole Goaso Municipality. He has experience in Information Communication Technology and keen interest in Human Resource Management, Project Management, Social Services Development and Community Development.
